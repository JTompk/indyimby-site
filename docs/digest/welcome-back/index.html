IndyIMBY is back — and this time it comes with receipts

Eleven years ago this site argued that Indianapolis should say yes to more housing. The argument hasn’t changed. The evidence just got a lot better.

Starting today, IndyIMBY runs on data: every petition filed with the Metropolitan Development Commission, Hearing Examiner, the Boards of Zoning Appeals, and the Plat Committee, scraped from public agendas each week, geocoded, and mapped on the Entitlement Tracker.

Every Monday, this digest will tell you what was filed, where it clusters, and which cases deserve a room full of neighbors saying yes.
