title: IndyIMBY is back — and this time it comes with receipts
date: 2026-07-06
summary: Relaunching the blog as a live database of every development filing in Indianapolis, updated weekly, plus a Monday digest of what it means.
---

Eleven years ago this site argued that Indianapolis should say yes to more
housing. The argument hasn't changed. The evidence just got a lot better.

Starting today, IndyIMBY runs on data: every petition filed with the
Metropolitan Development Commission, Hearing Examiner, the Boards of Zoning
Appeals, and the Plat Committee, scraped from public agendas each week,
geocoded, and mapped on the [Entitlement Tracker](https://map.indyimby.com).

Every Monday, this digest will tell you what was filed, where it clusters,
and which cases deserve a room full of neighbors saying *yes*.
