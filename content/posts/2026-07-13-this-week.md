1802 new filings hit the DMD dockets this week, with activity heaviest in Center Township. 649 rezonings lead the docket — land looking to become something else.

The full breakdown: 649 rezonings, 501 variance / special exceptions, 220 commitment / covenants, 122 approval / appeals, 105 modifications, 104 plat / subdivisions, 101 others.
Activity concentrated in Center (597), Washington (252), Perry (233), Warren (191), Wayne (143) — [see the map](https://map.indyimby.com).
